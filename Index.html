Morning hunt summary

—
This dashboard uses SunCalc for astronomical estimates and Open-Meteo for free weather data. Solunar windows are approximate planning aids, not guarantees of deer movement. Wind direction, access route, pressure, food, temperature changes and trail-camera observations should carry more weight. Tennessee general hunting hours for big game are generally 30 minutes before legal sunrise through 30 minutes after legal sunset; always check the current TWRA regulations and any WMA-specific rules before hunting.
